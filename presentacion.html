Transforming the Last Mile

Our transportation network is the backbone of our operation, but also our largest source of emissions. The transition to electric vehicles is not just an option; it is an urgent necessity for the health of our cities.

  • Total replacement of diesel vans with Rivian electric vans.
  • Reduction of noise and pollution in urban areas.
  • Smart charging infrastructure in all warehouses.
